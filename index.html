The Happs is a functional mobile application aimed to provide streamlined news content in a visually intriguing way.

Anyone can create content, post it on social media and call it news. We recognize this accessibility to publishing platforms is a huge problem — there appears to be an overwhelming amount of “news” being produced that people feel inundated and confused. In this, we saw opportunity.
